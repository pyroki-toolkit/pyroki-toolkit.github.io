A Modular Toolkit for

*Denotes Equal Contribution

TL;DR: PyRoki is a modular, extensible, and cross-platform toolkit for kinematic optimization.

We unify problems like inverse kinematics, trajectory optimization, and motion retargeting using composable kinematic variables and costs. PyRoki aims to support a broad variety of robots and tasks, and runs on CPU, GPU, and TPU.
